    Model Audit

    Why the ranker is defensible

    JD interpreter Turns the long-form JD into role evidence: retrieval, ranking, embeddings, vector infrastructure, evaluation, product depth, and logistics.
    Risk controls Down-weights keyword stuffing, services-only backgrounds, experience mismatches, stale candidates, and CV/speech-heavy profiles without IR fit.
    Behavior layer Uses Redrob signals like last active date, recruiter response, notice period, interview completion, verification, and GitHub signal.
    Reproducibility Streams the 100K pool, ranks deterministically, and writes the exact CSV format without network calls during the ranking step.
